What makes Dreamlayer unique?

Dreamlayer isn't just another wall light - it's layered art piece that blends depth, texture, and light into one display. By using stacked panels and programmable illumination, it creates shifting visual effects that feel alive, not static. Instead of being a flat glow on your wall, Dreamlayer changes as you move through the room , transforming ordinary walls into a living canvas.

How long does delivery take?

Each Dreamlayer is carefully packaged and shipped within 1-2 business days of your order. Standard delivery typically arrives within 5-7 business days in the U.S., with international shipping times varying by region. From checkout to doorstep, you'll receive real-time tracking updates so you can follow your Dreamlayer's journey.

What is the return policy?

We want every Dreamlayer to feel like it truly belongs in your space. If it doesn't, you have 30 days to return it in its original condition for a full refund - no questions, no hassle. Your experience should feel effortless from start to finish, and that includes making a return as a simple as placing the order.
