Roadmap and Updates

Concept & Optimization (Current Stage)
  • Defined the core vision: a layered wall display that blends art, light, and interactivity.
  • Optimized the design for durability, energy efficiency, and seamless user control.
  • Completed digital mockups and early render tests to refine the visual depth and lighting flow.
Prototype Build (Upcoming)
  • Produce the first working unit with layered panels and programmable illumination.
  • Conducted stress testing on mounting, heat distribution, and long-term brightness.
  • Gathered feedback on initial form factor and made adjustments for size and ease of installation.
Alpha Testing
  • Shared prototypes with a small closed group of testers.
  • Collected insights on usability, app control, and overall visual impact.
  • Idnetified needed improvements, including smoother light transitions and more intuitive controls.
Beta Test Run
  • Expanded testing to a wider group for real-world feedback across different environments.
  • Focusing on fine-tuning user experience: refining app interface, expanding preset themes, and ensuring reliable connectivity.
  • Gathering final impressions before locking in the production-ready version.
Launch Preparation
  • Final manufacturing adjustments based on beta results.
  • Preparing packaging, shipping logistics, and brand rollout materials.
  • Early access release for supports, followed by full public launched.
